quiz Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calibrate my camera? expand_more

To calibrate your camera, go to Settings > Camera Setup. Ensure you are in a well-lit room and stand approximately 3-5 feet away from the device. Follow the on-screen guides to align your body within the frame.

What signs are currently supported? expand_more

MUDRA currently supports over 500 common Indian Sign Language (ISL) gestures, including alphabets, numbers, and everyday phrases. We update our library monthly with new signs based on community feedback.

Is MUDRA compatible with mobile devices? expand_more

Yes, MUDRA is accessible via mobile web browsers on both iOS and Android. However, for the best 3D avatar performance and camera tracking, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer.
