About MUDRA

Bridging communication gaps with accessible, AI-driven Indian Sign Language technology.

Empowering conversations, one sign at a time.

Our Mission

Communication is a fundamental right, not a privilege.

MUDRA aims to empower the deaf and hard-of-hearing community by providing seamless, accessible, and real-time Indian Sign Language (ISL) translation tools.

We leverage state-of-the-art computer vision to recognize hand gestures and translate them into text and speech, breaking down the barriers that have historically isolated the deaf community from the digital world.

Real-Time Hand Tracking & 3D Avatars

MUDRA goes beyond simple text-to-speech. We utilize advanced skeletal tracking to interpret subtle finger movements. Additionally, our platform features lifelike 3D avatars that can sign back to users, creating a complete two-way communication loop.
